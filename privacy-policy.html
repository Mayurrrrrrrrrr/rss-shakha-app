Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the संघस्थान ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our application (the "App") and the corresponding website services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through the App, such as when you create an account, update your profile (including name, phone number, and branch details), or input data related to daily activities, attendance, and notices.

Because this App primarily functions as a web wrapper for our existing web platform (sanghasthan.yuktaa.com), the data processing practices are identical to those of the website.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionalities.
  • Manage Swayamsevak records and daily Shakha activities.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, notices, and administrative messages.
  • Generate reports and snapshots for authorized users.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data is strictly used for internal administrative purposes within the organization.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You can do this by contacting your local administrator or Mukhya Shikshak.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the administrative team.
